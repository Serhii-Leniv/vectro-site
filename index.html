One agent at a time is the bottleneck.

You can only babysit one terminal while it thinks. Vectro lets you start five, keep them from stepping on each other, and merge the one that got it right.

How it works

From a cold repo to merged work in three moves.

1

Open your repo

Point Vectro at any git project. Your layout and terminals are saved per workspace and restored when you come back.

2

Spin up agents

Every terminal runs your own CLI in its own branch + git worktree — up to nine, tiled automatically. Drag to reorder; the grid re-flows itself.

3

Review & merge

See who's working, idle, or waiting on you. Read each agent's diff and merge into your base — or discard the branch — without leaving the canvas.

True isolation

Diff & merge in-app
